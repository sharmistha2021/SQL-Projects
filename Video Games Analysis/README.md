## 🎮 When Was the Golden Era of Video Games? (SQL Data Analysis Project)

Video games are one of the largest entertainment industries in the world. The global gaming market is projected to exceed **$300 billion by 2027** (Mordor Intelligence).

With such massive financial stakes, game publishers constantly compete to create the next big hit. But an interesting question remains:

👉 Are video games actually getting better over time, or has the golden age of gaming already passed?

---

## 📊 Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed critic scores, user scores, and sales data for the top 400 best-selling video games released since 1977.

The goal was to identify whether there is a “golden era” of video games by exploring:

- Years with the highest critic ratings  
- Years most appreciated by users    
- Alignment between commercial success and review scores  

---

## 🔍 Key Objective

To determine whether there is a specific period in gaming history where:
- Games were consistently better rated  
- Users and critics agreed on quality  
- Sales performance peaked alongside quality  

---

## 📌 What This Project Explores

- Identifying top-performing years in gaming history  
- Comparing critic vs user preferences  
- Linking game success with sales performance
